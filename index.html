About Me

    Hi! I am a Ph.D. student of the ECE department at NUS, supervised by Prof. Vincent Y. F. Tan and Dr. Jiashi Feng. I'm also working closely with Dr. Jingfeng Zhang and Prof. Masashi Sugiyama from RIKEN-AIP.
    My research interests include Robust Machine Learning, Neural ODE, and Image Processing. Formerly, I received M. Sc (Electrical Engineering) from NUS, B.Eng (Electrical Engineering), and B. Sc (Applied Mathematics) from Beihang University (BUAA).
