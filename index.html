About Me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. candidate of the ECE department at NUS, fortunately working with Prof. Vincent Y. F. Tan and Prof. Jiashi Feng. My research interests include robust Machine Learning and Image Processing. Formerly, I received M. Sc (Electrical Engineering) from NUS and B.Eng (Electrical Engineering) & B. Sc (Applied Mathematics) from Beihang University (BUAA).
